Who am I?

Hello, I'm Alice! I'm happy to be able to share a little bit about myself with you.
Below are some of the key things that make up who I am.

  • Programmer

    I discovered my love for programming through my investment experience and my work in data operations at a startup.I watched private equity firms dominate the industry through quantitative analysis and saw tech companies expand their market share by leveraging technology. These experiences sparked my interest in transitioning to programming.

  • Former Investor

    Before transitioning into the tech world, I worked in private equity, specializing in investing in top-performing private funds. I had the opportunity to research and evaluate China’s leading private equity firms, which provided me with a strong understanding of financial markets and investment strategies. My experience as an investor shaped my problem-solving approach and gave me a solid foundation in strategic thinking and analysis, which I now apply in my programming career.

  • Lifelong Learner

    I am a firm believer in lifelong learning. My curiosity about the world drives me to continuously expand my knowledge. Whether it’s tech, philosophy, or the arts, I am always seeking new information and skills. Currently, I’m focused on mastering programming languages and exploring new frameworks, as well as diving into areas like machine learning and artificial intelligence.

  • Owner of Three Cats

    I share my home with three wonderful cats, who have taught me the importance of patience, responsibility, and unconditional love. They are an integral part of my life, and their companionship brings comfort and joy. Having pets has made me more compassionate and appreciative of the simple pleasures in life.

  • Immigrant to the U.S.

    I moved to the United States to start a new chapter in my life, where I am re-learning the language, applying for a green card, and diving deep into new technologies. This transition has been a significant challenge but also a rewarding journey of personal and professional growth. I am excited to explore new opportunities and build a fresh career in the tech industry.

  • Anime and Music Lover

    Anime and music are also an important part of who I am. I enjoy Studio Ghibli films and Avatar: The Last Airbender. My taste in music is broader; I believe music is a vessel for emotions, and all human emotions are impactful.
